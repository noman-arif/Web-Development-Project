Favorite Personalities

Imran Khan

Imran Khan, born October 5, 1952, in Lahore, is a former Pakistani cricketer and current politician. He led Pakistan to a Cricket World Cup victory in 1992 and founded the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) party in 1996. He became Prime Minister in 2018, focusing on anti-corruption and economic reforms.

Imran Riaz

Imran Riaz Khan is a prominent Pakistani journalist and TV anchor known for his bold and outspoken commentary. He gained popularity for his investigative journalism and critical stance on various political issues. Khan is active on social media, where he shares his insights and engages with a large audience.

Muhammad Ali Jinnah

Quaid-e-Azam, meaning "Great Leader," is the title of Muhammad Ali Jinnah, the founder of Pakistan. Born on December 25, 1876, in Karachi, Jinnah led the movement for an independent Pakistan and became its first Governor-General in 1947. He is celebrated for his pivotal role in creating Pakistan and his leadership until his death on September 11, 1948.

Engin Altan

Engin Altan Düzyatan, born on July 26, 1979, in Izmir, Turkey, is a renowned Turkish actor. He is best known for his role as Ertuğrul Bey in the acclaimed historical drama series "Diriliş: Ertuğrul" (2014-2019), which brought him international fame.

Abdul Sttar

Abdul Sattar Edhi, born on January 1, 1928, in Bantva, British India, was a renowned Pakistani philanthropist and humanitarian. He founded the Edhi Foundation, which runs a vast network of free hospitals, orphanages, homeless shelters, and ambulance services across Pakistan. Known for his tireless dedication to helping the poor and needy, Edhi became a symbol of compassion and service.

Dr Abdul Qadeer

Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan, born April 1, 1936, was a prominent Pakistani nuclear scientist known as the father of Pakistan's nuclear program. He played a pivotal role in developing Pakistan's nuclear capabilities, leading to successful nuclear tests in 1998. His contributions significantly enhanced Pakistan's defense capabilities.

Mustak Ahmed

Mushtaq Ahmed Ghani (Urdu: مشتاق احمد غنی; born 30 October 1956) is a Pakistani politician who served as Speaker of the Provincial Assembly of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa from 2018 till 2024.

Dr Israr Ahmed

Dr. Israr Ahmed (1932–2010) was a prominent Pakistani Islamic scholar, theologian, and founder of Tanzeem-e-Islami. He was known for his profound understanding of the Quran and his advocacy for Islamic education and ideology, including the establishment of an Islamic state governed by Sharia law.
